Peanut Butter Cookie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Stir ingredients together
  2. Roll dough into 1-inch balls
  3. Place balls 1-inch apart on ungreased baking sheet
  4. Flatten balls with tines of fork
  5. Bake at 325 degrees for 15 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
